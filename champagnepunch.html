Champagne Punch



    Ingredients

  • 2 bottles of Champagne
  • 1 bottle of Moscato
  • 4 cups of sparkling water
  • 1.5 cups of cognac
  • Grapes to garnish (optional)

Makes 15 servings

Nutrition Information
Amount per serving 1 cup
Calories 206
Total Fat 0g
Saturated Fat 0g
Total Cholesterol 0mg
Sodium 14mg
Total Carbohydrates 12g
Fiber 3g
Sugar 5g
Protein 1g

    Instructions

  1. Stir together Champagne, Moscato, Cognac, and sparkling water.
  2. Add grapes on a skewer if desired.

TIP: Make sure liquids are chilled before mixing together.

Please drink responsibly.
For people 21+ years of age.
