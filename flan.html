Flan



    Ingredients

  • 1 cup powdered Monk Fruit sweetner
  • 6 eggs
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 2 cups heavy cream
Nutrition Information
Amount per serving 1 slice
Calories 331
Total Fat 33g
Total Carbohydrates 3g
Fiber 0.1g
Sugar 3g
Protein 5g

    Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Combine 1/2 cup of monk fruit and 1/4 cup of water in a saucepan over low heat.
  3. Heat until it is dissolved.
  4. Increase heat until syrup boils, then simmer for about 20 minutes until syrup turns gold.
  5. Quickly pour syrup into around 8" x 8" baking pan and tilt until syrup is evenly spread.
  6. Whisk egg yolks together.
  7. In another saucepan, heat the heavy cream, remainder of the monk fruit sweetner and salt over medium heat.
  8. Remove from heat and whisk in the vanilla extract once bubble start to form.
  9. Slowly combine mixture with the egg yolks while constantly whisking.
  10. Using a finw sieve, pour custard into the pan.
  11. Place the pan into a larger pan.
  12. Slowly pour water into the larger pan without pouring it into the 8" x 8" pan.
  13. Bake for about 25mins until flan is almost set.
  14. Remove from oven and let cool for about 2hrs.
  15. Refrigerate overnight.
