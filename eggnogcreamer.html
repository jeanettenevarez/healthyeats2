Eggnog Creamer



    Ingredients

  • 2 cups of half-and-half*
  • 1 tsp. of rum extract
  • 1/4 tsp. of ground nutmeg
  • 2-3 tbsp. of honey
  • 1/2 tsp. of vanilla
  • coffee of choice

Makes 36 servings

*SUB: You can use 1 cup of whole milk and 1 cup of heavy cream.

Nutrition Information
Amount per serving 1 tbsp.
Calories 18
Total Fat 1g
Saturated Fat 1g
Total Cholesterol 5mg
Sodium 8mg
Total Carbohydrates 1g
Fiber 0g
Sugar 1g
Protein 0g

    Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a 1-qt. jar.
  2. Seal and store in the refirgerator for up to 2 weeks.
  3. Stir before adding it to the coffee.
  4. Enjoy!

Please drink responsibly.
For people 21+ years of age.
