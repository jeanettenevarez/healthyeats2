Cheesecake



    Ingredients for the crust

  • 1.5 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup powdered sweetner
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 6 tbsp. Butter (melted)

    • Ingredients for the filling

  • 6 8oz cream cheese
  • 2 cups powdered sweetner
  • 5 eggs
  • 8 oz. sour cream
  • 1 tbsp. vanilla extract

    • Ingredients for the sauce

  • 2 tbsp monk fruit sweetner
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp lemon
Nutrition Information
Amount per serving 1 slice
Calories 325
Total Fat 31g
Total Carbohydrates 5g
Fiber 1g
Sugar 2g
Protein 7g

    Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 325Â°F.
  2. Crust: Combine dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Mix in the butter.
  4. But crust mixture into a 10 x 4 inch pan and spread evenly.
  5. Refrigerate for about 20mins.
  6. Beat the cream cheese into fluffy.
  7. Add in sweetner little by little.
  8. Add eggs, vanilla, and the sour cream and mix until well blended.
  9. Pour cheesecake filling into the crust.
  10. Bake for about 50mins and let rest for about 30mins with oven off.
  11. Rest for another hour and refrigerate for 8 hours.
