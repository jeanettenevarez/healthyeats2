Chocolate Chip Cookies



    Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups ground almond flour
  • 6 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp. stevia glycerite
  • 1/4 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
Nutrition Information
Amount per serving 1 cookie
Calories 180
Total Fat 16g
Saturated Fat 6g
Sodium 59mg
Total Carbohydrates 6g
Fiber 3g
Protein 4g

    Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Mix butter, stevia, vanilla, and egg with a spatula.
  3. Add in flour little by little until well blended.
  4. Mix in the salt and baking soda.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Line a cookie tray with parchment paper.
  7. Use a tablespoon to scoop up dough to form balls.
  8. Place around 2in apart from each other.
  9. Bake the cookies from 15-20mins or until they are a light brown.
  10. Let cool. Enjoy!
