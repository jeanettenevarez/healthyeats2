Capirotada



    Ingredients for the bread

  • 1.5 cups almond flour
  • 2 eggs
  • Pinch of stevia
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. baking powder
  • 2 tbsp. coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp. heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp. active yeast
  • 3/4 cups water

    • Ingredients for the filling

  • 2.5 cups almond milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp. stevia
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

    • Toppings

  • 1 cup of raisins
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 cup of peeled peanuts
  • Sprinkles
Nutrition Information
Amount per serving 1 serving
Calories 268
Total Fat 19g
Total Carbohydrates 7g
Fiber 4g
Protein 5g

    Instructions

  1. Butter the bottom of a cooking pan and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the almond milk, heavy cream, egg, stevia, and salt and stir until it simmers.
  3. Set aside.
  4. Slice the loaf of bread and place a layer of bread into the cooking pan.
  5. Pour the mixture into the first layer of bread and add shredded on top.
  6. Add another layer of bread, add the liquid mixture and cheese.
  7. Place saucepan over low heat until it simmers.
  8. Once the liquid is absorbed into the bread, remove from heat and let it cool.
  9. Once it has cooled, add toppings to your liking.
